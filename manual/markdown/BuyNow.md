## Summary (lead paragraphs)
Thank you for considering purchasing 010 Editor. We are committed to providing a top-quality, professional text/hex/disk editor with a full set of powerful editing tools.

After using this program for 30 days, you must purchase a license to continue using the software. Licenses can be purchased using all major credit cards, cheque, PayPal, fax, or phone. To purchase, click ' Tools > Licensing... ' and click the Purchase a license of 010 Editor link in the Licensing dialog, or click the ' Help > Buy Now... ' menu option. You may also directly visit ' https://www.sweetscape.com/store/ ' with any web browser. Once at the website, follow the on screen instructions to complete your purchase. All purchases are secure and backed by a 60-day money-back guarantee.

After your purchase you will receive a license that must be entered in the Licensing dialog (this dialog used to be called the Register dialog in older versions of 010 Editor). The Licensing dialog automatically displays if your evaluation period has ended and can be accessed on the menu from ' Tools > Licensing... ' or from the Welcome or About dialogs. The following is a list of the fields in the Licensing dialog:

Name - Enter your name exactly as you entered it when purchasing the product. Your license will only work for your name. License - Enter the license or password that was emailed to you when you purchased the product. Clicking the Activate License button will test if your entered name and license are valid. If you experience any problems, please visit ' https://www.sweetscape.com/support/ '. A number of links are also available at the bottom of the dialog: Purchase a license of 010 Editor - Visits the online store where you can purchase a license of 010 Editor. Purchase an extension to support/maintenance - If your support/maintenance period has expired (see the Support/Maintenance information below), clicking this link allows you to purchase an extension to your maintenance so you can continue to have access to support and free upgrades for 010 Editor. I lost my license code - Retrieves a lost license using our webpage ' https://www.sweetscape.com/010editor/retrieve_password.html '. You will be required to enter the e-mail address used to purchase 010 Editor. Remove my license from this machine - Removes the current license from this computer. Warning: If you remove your license you will no longer to be able to use this software until you enter another valid license. Hide my license - Usually the Licensing dialog displays the last entered license in the License field. If this option is selected then XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX is displayed instead of the license. Hiding the license is recommended if the license is entered on a public computer. The only way to unhide the license is to enter the license again into the License field and click Activate License . Clicking Activate License automatically unhides the license and click Hide my license again to hide the license. Below the list of links is the Support/Maintenance expiration date (this is displayed only after a valid license is entered).
